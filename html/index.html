When the subject is immobile, EGW has all the solutions.


The foundation in 1932 took place at the time of the economic discovery of the interior of Paraná. The Pelho family, traditional in the region, invested all their savings in this new initiative, revolutionary for the time. Founder Eduardo Simões Pelho, endowed with a particular administrative vision, guided the company's business for more than 50 years, many of them alongside his son E. S. Pelho Filho, current CEO. The company name is inspired by the family name. The company's growth was virtually instantaneous. In the first 5 years, it already served 18 countries. It hit the mark of 100 countries in just 15 years of existence. To date, it has served 740 million different users on billions of different requests. The growth in the number of employees is also astonishing. Today, it is the largest employer in Brazil, but even after only 5 years of its existence, it already had 30 thousand employees. Outside Brazil, there are 240,000 employees, in addition to the 890,000 Brazilians at the Jacarezinho facilities and offices across the country. Given the company's economic importance to Brazil, the Pelho family has already received several awards, honors and decorations. All the presidents of Brazil have already visited the Mirror Fashion facilities, as well as presidents of the European Union, Asia and the UN Secretary General.
